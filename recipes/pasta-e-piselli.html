Pasta i Picelli

Pasta with peas and bacon!

It's light and low in calories, but is very nutritious. It is also very budget friendly and convenient

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil
  • 200 g/8oz Pancetta or Bacon
  • 1 leek or 3 green onions
  • 250g/8oz Ditali or Ditalini pasta
  • 3 cups Hot chicken stock
  • 2 cups frozen green peas
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • 2-3 fresh mint leaves (optional)
  • 2-3 basil leaves

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large and deep pan and add the diced pancetta or bacon and cook until most of the fat is rendered, add the sliced leek and saute over low heat for 5 minutes. Then add the pasta and hot stock, bring to a boil and cook for 6 minutes, stirring once in a while to ensure the pasta is not sticking to the bottom.
  2. Add the peas and cook for 2 minutes longer. Then stir in the grated cheese and the fresh herbs, season with salt to taste.
  3. Serve with additional Parmesan cheese.
